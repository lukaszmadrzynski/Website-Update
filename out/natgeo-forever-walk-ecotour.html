Highlights

Walk a renowned section of the "Forever Walk: China" trail, as documented by National Geographic. Immerse yourself in the very landscapes and cultural encounters that captivated the world in this celebrated documentary series.

Follow a part of Pulitzer Prize winner Paul Salopek's epic global walk. Reflect on humanity and our planet as you trace his path through culturally rich Yunnan, connecting with the world through mindful exploration on foot.

Discover the remarkable biodiversity of Wenhai Wetlands alongside a local conservationist. Learn about unique species and delicate ecosystems, witnessing firsthand the importance of conservation efforts in this stunning natural haven.

Experience genuine Naxi hospitality with an overnight stay in Wenhai village. Share in local customs, observe daily life, and connect with the warm community, gaining unique insights into their traditions and way of living.

Uncover the mysteries of Tiger Cave, a fascinating local geological wonder. Explore its intriguing rock formations and delve into the legends surrounding this natural site, adding an element of adventure to your ecotour.

Journey through charming minority villages and tranquil hamlets, each with its own unique character. Observe diverse architectural styles, local traditions, and the peaceful rhythm of rural life in Yunnan's diverse communities.

Hike through the untouched wilderness of Jade Dragon Snow Mountain Nature Reserve accompanied by a local forest ranger. Explore pristine landscapes, learn about the region’s ecology, and experience the raw beauty of protected nature.

Immerse yourself in Yi culture with an overnight stay in Runangu village. Engage with welcoming villagers, learn about their distinct customs, and participate in daily life, fostering genuine cultural exchange and understanding.

Witness the extraordinary biodiversity of ancient forests within Jade Dragon Snow Mountain. Discover a rich tapestry of plant and animal life, appreciating the ecological significance of Yunnan's primeval forests and their vital role in the ecosystem.

Gather around a cozy bonfire with local villagers for an evening of cultural connection. Share stories, laughter, and warmth under the starry Yunnan sky, forging memorable bonds and experiencing genuine local hospitality.

Stand in awe of the majestic Jade Dragon Snow Mountain, its towering peaks dominating the landscape. Capture breathtaking panoramic views and feel the powerful presence of this iconic natural wonder, a true highlight of Yunnan.

Journey alongside renowned conservation and ecotourism experts deeply rooted in Yunnan’s landscapes. Their expertise ensures safety, while their passion for preserving ecosystems and sharing cultural stories turns the journey into an enriching, unforgettable adventure.
